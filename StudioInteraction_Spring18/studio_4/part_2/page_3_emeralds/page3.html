EMERALD MINING

Human knowledge of emeralds and emerald mining is thought to date back to ancient Egypt. While emeralds rank highly on the hardness scale, they are quite brittle. As of late, most emeralds are treated to hide the number of inclusions or internal fractures within them, which are a more than common trait. This treatment has been known to happen with oil, particularly cedar oil, but recently polymers have been used.
